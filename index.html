About Us

Chicago's Premier Charter Experience

TMarK is a Chicago-based marine lifestyle brand providing premium charter services, dock-stay accommodations, and RIB vessel sales. Co-founded by Captain Emmanuel 'Kip' Chepkwony and Tiffany, we are Chicago's go-to waterfront experience provider.

What began as a passion for Chicago's waterfront has evolved into a professional marine lifestyle brand built on four core pillars: Credibility, Reliability, Intimacy, and Scalability. Every detail matters, and quality is non-negotiable.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

TMarK offers three core services: premium charter cruises on Lake Michigan (TMarK RIB from $225/hr, Ndinda Cruiser from $275/hr, Emily Faye sailing from $175/hr, Jet Ski from $95/30min), Airbnb dock-stay overnight accommodations aboard the Emily Faye ($275–350/night), and RIB vessel sales through our partnership network ($5,500–11,000/unit). Plus 15+ curated Chill Harbor Experiences!

Our fleet includes four vessels: the TMarK (24-foot RIB with fast motor for Chicago skyline cruises, up to 6 guests), the Ndinda (29-foot Crownline cabin cruiser for adventure charters, up to 6 guests), the Emily Faye (29-foot C&C sailboat for romantic overnight and weekend stays on Lake Michigan), and Rita (jet ski for thrill rides). All vessels are maintained to USCG standards.

The Emily Faye dock-stay is a romantic overnight or weekend experience aboard our 29-foot C&C sailboat on Lake Michigan. Perfect for couples, each booking includes a welcome briefing, cozy cabin with clean linens, and the option for a sunset sail. Accommodates 2–4 guests.

Not at all! All charters are captained by USCG-certified Captain Kip and trained crew. You and your guests simply sit back and enjoy the ride. Safety briefings are provided before every departure.

Safety is non-negotiable at TMarK. If weather conditions are unsafe, we will reschedule your charter at no additional charge. We monitor forecasts closely and keep you informed. The Ndinda cabin cruiser offers a covered cabin area for light rain.

Charters depart from our dock in the Chicago harbor system. Exact departure location, parking instructions, and a welcome briefing are provided upon booking confirmation. The Emily Faye dock-stay is located at our primary slip.

Our primary charter season runs from May through October. Dock-stay bookings on the Emily Faye begin in June. RIB sales inquiries are welcome year-round.
